# HuggingFace

Industry-leading platform for transformer models and machine learning. Provides access to thousands of pre-trained models and tools for fine-tuning, deployment, and collaboration.

## Key Features

- **Transformers Library**: State-of-the-art NLP, vision, and audio models
- **Model Hub**: 100,000+ pre-trained models
- **Datasets**: Large collection of ML datasets
- **Fine-tuning**: Easy model adaptation and training
- **Inference API**: Deploy models at scale

## My Experience

Used extensively for LLM fine-tuning, model deployment, and working with transformer architectures in astronomical data analysis and NLP tasks.
